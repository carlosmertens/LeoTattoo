Studio

Our studio is a space for art and tattoos, we specialise in realism, black & grey, and colour.

The studio’s atmosphere is very important to us: customers are warmly greeted and should feel free to take advantage of a long consultation beforehand. We respond to our client’s ideas over a cup of coffee and turn those inspirations into realistic projects. Guest artists from all over the world visit us to realise and further their art. At the end of the day we want our customers to be satisfied and hold their experience at our studio as a dear memory. Leonardo originally comes from South America, we try and incorporate that Latin American flair and warmth into our space.

Send us a noncommittal inquiry about your desired tattoo and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
